How to Use

This map shows earthquakes based on selected date range and filters.

1. Use Brush Mode to drag-select a region. Use Pan mode to pan across the map.

2. You can use the fullscreen buttons to make the map larger. Click again to make the map at its orginial size.

3. You can change the map type under the mode button with a drop down once clicked.

4. If an earthquake is clicked, red highlights indicate what categories the earthquake falls in the graphs.

5. The animate button allows you to see the earthquakes over the period of the data set.

6. You can stop at any time! Feel free to hover over the timeline to get specifics.
